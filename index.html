Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2025

At Monk Musician, we strive to provide high-quality music, mindfulness, and cultural education experiences. However, we understand that circumstances may arise where you need to request a refund. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are granted for our services, including online courses, events, and digital products.

  1. General Refund Policy

    2. Refund eligibility varies based on the type of service purchased. Please review the specific policies below:

    1. Online Courses & Digital Programs

      • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase.
      • To be eligible, you must not have completed more than 20% of the course material.
      • No refunds will be granted for courses that include downloadable materials, unless a technical issue prevents access.

    2. Live and Virtual Events

      • Refunds for paid events are available if requested at least 48 hours before the event start time.
      • If an event is canceled or rescheduled by Monk Musician, you will be offered a full refund or the option to attend the rescheduled event.
      • No-shows are not eligible for refunds.

    3. Memberships & Subscriptions

      • Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be canceled at any time.
      • Annual memberships may be eligible for a partial refund if canceled within the first 30 days, minus any used benefits.

    4. Physical Products & Merchandise

      • Physical products (e.g., branded merchandise) must be returned in unused condition within 14 days of delivery for a refund.
      • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

  2. Refund Process

    To request a refund, please follow these steps:

    1. Contact us at support@Monkmusician.com with your order details and reason for the refund request.
    2. Our team will review your request and respond within 5 business days.
    3. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

  3. Non-Refundable Items & Services

    The following are not eligible for refunds:

    • Completed courses or content that has been fully accessed.
    • Downloadable digital products (e.g., sheet music, guided meditations, or PDFs).
    • Private coaching sessions that were canceled with less than 24-hour notice.
    • Gift cards or promotional discounts.

  4. Contact Information

    For any refund-related inquiries, please reach out to:

    Email: support@monkmusician.com
    Website: Monkmusician.com

    By purchasing from Monkmusician.com, you acknowledge and agree to this Refund Policy. We appreciate your understanding and support!
